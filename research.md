# Science of science

Science is increasingly a collective effort, researchers should team up with other colleagues to reach further shores that they would be able to reach in isolation. Despite the preconception of science being developed in the most rational manner, since scholars are also social beings the way these collaborations is highly influenced by the echoes of social norms. Different societies give birth to different collaboration structures, some individuals might have a longlasting initial burst thanks to being in the correct launching environment, persistent societal inequalities might hindrance the growth and access of certain demographics within the scientific workforce. 

# Online collective organization

Online platforms have become one of the main venues for the exchange of ideas, information and collective organization. By their nature, they offer the opportunity to connect instantly to millions of users across the globe which in turn, can be one of its biggest threats as we have seen with the rapid spread of misinformation, coordinated harassment campaigns and the undermining of the modern democratic systems. Network science and LLMs allow us to analyze both, the structure and content of these platforms identifying the ideal growth structures for coordinated behaviour and the underground passages through which political framings are passed using apolitical communities to reach a larger body of population.
